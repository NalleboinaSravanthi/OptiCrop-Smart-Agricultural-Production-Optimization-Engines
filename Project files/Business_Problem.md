# Specify the Business Problem

## Epic 1: Define Problem and Understanding

## Overview

Modern agriculture faces numerous challenges, including poor crop
selection, inefficient use of natural resources, unpredictable climatic
conditions, and declining agricultural productivity. Farmers often rely
on traditional farming practices or personal experience when selecting
crops, which may not always align with current soil and environmental
conditions. As a result, unsuitable crop selection can lead to low
yields, increased production costs, financial losses, and inefficient
utilization of water, fertilizers, and other agricultural resources.

The **OptiCrop: Smart Agricultural Production Optimization Engine** aims
to address these challenges by providing an intelligent crop
recommendation system powered by machine learning. The system analyzes
key soil and environmental parameters to recommend the most suitable
crop for cultivation, enabling farmers to make informed and data-driven
decisions.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Business Problem

The primary business problem addressed by OptiCrop is the lack of a
reliable and intelligent decision-support system for crop selection.
Many farmers face difficulties in identifying crops that are best suited
to their land due to variations in soil nutrients and changing climatic
conditions. Incorrect crop selection reduces productivity, increases
operational costs, and affects farmers' income.

The proposed solution minimizes these risks by analyzing agricultural
data and generating accurate crop recommendations tailored to the user's
soil and environmental conditions.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Key Agricultural Parameters

The system considers the following parameters while recommending crops:

-   Nitrogen (N)
-   Phosphorous (P)
-   Potassium (K)
-   Temperature
-   Humidity
-   Soil pH
-   Rainfall
-   Seasonal Conditions

These parameters significantly influence crop growth, productivity, and
overall farming success.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Stakeholders

The proposed system is beneficial for:

-   Farmers
-   Agricultural Researchers
-   Agribusiness Organizations
-   Government Agencies
-   Policymakers
-   Agricultural Extension Officers

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Objectives

-   Improve crop selection accuracy.
-   Increase agricultural productivity and profitability.
-   Optimize the use of water, fertilizers, and soil nutrients.
-   Reduce crop failure caused by unsuitable environmental conditions.
-   Support sustainable and precision farming practices.
-   Enable data-driven agricultural decision-making.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Expected Outcomes

By implementing the OptiCrop system, farmers can identify the most
suitable crop for cultivation based on scientific analysis rather than
guesswork. The solution is expected to improve crop yields, reduce
resource wastage, enhance sustainability, and contribute to long-term
agricultural development.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Conclusion

Clearly defining the business problem establishes the foundation for the
OptiCrop project. Understanding agricultural challenges, stakeholder
needs, and environmental factors enables the development of an
intelligent crop recommendation system that improves farming efficiency,
supports sustainable agriculture, and promotes informed decision-making.
